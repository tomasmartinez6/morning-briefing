## 2026-07-14 — Tuesday

**Full issue:** https://tomasmartinez6.github.io/morning-briefing/#2026-07-14

**AI & Technology** — OpenAI proposed giving the U.S. government a 5%
equity stake (~$42.6B at $852B valuation), with Sam Altman pitching it
to Trump, Lutnick, and Bessent. OpenAI also launched GPT-5.6 (Sol/Terra/Luna),
which became the default in Microsoft 365 Copilot — though Microsoft
simultaneously announced it is replacing OpenAI/Anthropic models with in-house
AI in some Copilot apps. Google unveiled Gemini Enterprise at Cloud Next '26,
focused on enterprise AI-agent fleets.

**Stock Market** — S&P 500 -0.79%, Nasdaq -1.55%, Dow -0.26%. Bank earnings
were exceptional (JPM Q2 EPS $7.70 vs. $5.59 est.; Goldman +13% revenue
forecast on SpaceX IPO underwriting ~$500M fees; IB revenue sector-wide +26%),
but markets sold off on the formal U.S. Hormuz blockade announced 4 PM ET.

**Markets & Economy** — June CPI: headline -0.1% MoM, YoY down to ~3.9%
from 4.2%. Core held flat at ~2.9% YoY. Improvement driven almost entirely
by gas prices falling during a brief Hormuz ceasefire now ended. Fed pause
likely through September; rate-cut expectations priced out.

**World / Geopolitics** — U.S. formally blockaded Iranian shipping through
the Strait of Hormuz at 4 PM ET. Iran struck two UAE tankers with cruise
missiles Monday (1 killed). Strait crossings fell ~85% last week (22 ships).
Trump proposed 20% toll on cargo using U.S. naval protection through the Strait.

**Venezuela** — Six months post-Maduro capture: oil production at 7-year
high, exports to U.S. +192% (avg ~330K bpd). Economy ~80% contracted
2013-2025, showing early stabilization but triple-digit inflation persists.
Delcy Rodriguez acting president; Gonzalez/Machado pushing for transition;
Chavista institutions still in place, no election date set.

**Venezuelan History (education)** — Period: Chavez-to-Maduro Transition,
2012-2013. Angle: Chavez's Dec 2012 televised designation of Maduro as
successor; Maduro's background as bus driver/union organizer; Chavez's
death March 5, 2013; razor-thin 50.6%/49.1% (235K vote margin) presidential
election win over Capriles; Capriles's recount demand rejected; first year:
56% inflation, shortages, political fragility revealed.

**American Business History (education)** — Era 1: The Gilded Age & J.P. Morgan.
Week of 2026-07-13. Angle: The Panic of 1907 — Knickerbocker Trust failure,
Morgan's overnight return from Richmond, private-central-bank operation from
34th St mansion, library lockdown of trust company presidents until $25M pool
signed at 5 AM, and how Morgan's indispensability made the public case for
the Federal Reserve Act (signed Dec 1913, 8 months after his death).
